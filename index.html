Features

Music

You can listen to any of your favourite music with your friends in your server. MiMo can play any song in your server really fast and at high quality. All of it's features are free...

Reaction Controls

Can control the music player and volume in simple way, don't need prefix and command all time,simply control the music and volume by reaction control...

High Speed

MiMo Bot were working in high speed internet, so don't worry about the bot activities..You can simply setup this bot.

Commands

  • ,play
    To play Music from You Tube or Spotify.
    Can use music link or music title after this command.

    aliases : ,p

  • ,playalbum
    To play spotify album.
    Can use album link or album title after this command.

    aliases: ,palbum ,pal
  • ,playnext
    To add the song in queue as upnext.
    Can use music link or title after this command.

    aliases: ,pnext ,pn
  • ,nowplaying 
    Show the now playing music and can control the music and volume.

    aliases: ,np
  • ,queue
    To show the whole queue.

    aliases: ,q
  • ,remove
    To remove a song in the queue.
    Just enter the number of the song show in the queue.

    aliases: ,rem ,rm
  • ,clear
    To clears the queue. Does not stop playback.
  • ,pause
    To pause the Player.
    Just a command, can use this command as reaction in Music Player.

    aliases : ,ps

  • ,resume
    To resume the Player.
    It also just a command, can use this command as reaction in Music Player.
  • ,skip
    To skip the music.
  • ,skipto
    To skip the song to preferred song.

    aliases : ,rs

  • ,stop
    To stop the music and clear the queue.

    aliases : ,dc ,disconnect ,end

  • ,repeat
    To loop the entire queue.

    aliases: ,loop ,l
  • ,repeatone
    To loop one song.

    aliases: ,loopone ,lo
  • ,volume
    To show the volume or increase or decrease the volume.

    aliases: ,v ,vol
  • ,lyrics
    To show the lyrics of the current playing song.
    Or show the lyrics by song title.
  • ,top
    To show the most played song in the server.
